DAMASCUS

The capital of the Syrian Arab Republic and the center of Damascus governorate.
It is one of the oldest cities in the world with an uninterrupted history nearly eleven thousand years ago
and the oldest city - the capital of the world. It became the capital of the region of Syria since 635.
There are several theories to explain the meaning of the name of Damascus
which is spread widely because the ancient word is Semitic in the sense of the land of watering.
This is due to the city's geographical location in a fertile branches.
The Damascus name was limited to the Old City until the Middle Ages, when the city was expanded outside.
Damascus currently consists of 15 residential areas connected to its surroundings is known as Greater Damascus.
